International Alliance | Education
"Belt and Road" Film Education Alliance
In response to the initiative put forward by General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee to "strengthen international cooperation, jointly build the 'Belt and Road Initiative,' and achieve win-win development," and to promote film professional education and the social popularization of film culture in countries along the Belt and Road and other relevant countries, and to foster the development of young film talents from various countries, the "Belt and Road International Film Education Alliance," initiated and hosted by the Central Academy of Drama, was established on November 28th at our Changping campus.
Symposium | Broadcasting & Hosting
The Second National Symposium on Broadcasting and Hosting Professional Education
The "Second National Symposium on Broadcasting and Hosting Professional Education," organized by the Central Academy of Drama and hosted by its Department of Film and Television, was held from May 29th to 30th at the Changping campus of the Central Academy of Drama. Retrospective | Film History
Commemorating the 130th Anniversary of Ouyang Yuqian's Birth | Ouyang Yuqian Film Retrospective
2019 marked the 130th anniversary of the birth of Ouyang Yuqian, a renowned Chinese playwright, drama educator, early film artist, dance theorist, and the first president of the Central Academy of Drama. To commemorate this rare artistic polymath in the history of modern Chinese art, the Central Academy of Drama held a series of commemorative events.
